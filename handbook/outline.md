## Design principle (from the tasking)
The Playbook is **not a tool tutorial.** It must carry the core logic: efficiency gains
come when **AI is paired with process redesign, organizational change, workforce analytics,
and a deliberate plan for reinvesting recovered time** — capacity refocused toward
critically manned, operationally focused work. Framing throughout: this is about
**efficiency and mission capacity, not manpower reduction.**

## Working draft structure (to validate with Lead)
1. **Front matter** — title, purpose, how to use this Playbook, audience (all echelons)
2. **Why now** — the capacity problem and what "good" looks like
3. **Foundations** — what today's AI/LLMs can and can't do (plain language)
4. **From task to capacity** — pairing AI with process redesign + reinvestment of recovered time
5. **Adoption by echelon** — individual → team → unit guidance and examples
6. **Guardrails** — security, policy, data readiness, appropriate use
7. **Measuring it** — simple measures of effectiveness (MOE) for adoption and impact
8. **Playbooks/plays** — concrete, scalable adoption patterns
9. **Glossary** — sanitized subset of the canonical terms
